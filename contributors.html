Why we built RISE

RISE started as a simple idea — what if IMCA students had a single, beautiful platform to track their studies, access old papers and never feel lost in their academic journey?

What began as a small side project by Priyanshu grew into a fully-featured platform built by a passionate team of students who understood the struggle first-hand.
